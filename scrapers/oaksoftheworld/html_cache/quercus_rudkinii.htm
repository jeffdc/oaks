  Quercus x rudkinii
Author Britt. 1882 Bull. Torrey Bot. Club 9: 10-12
Synonyms marilandica x phellos
= dubia Ashe 1894, non Trel.
Local names
Range Eastern USA to Texas
Growth habit
Leaves very variable, even on the same twig; apex obtuse; base rounded or weakly cordate; margin entire or with obtuse lobes; young leaves pubescent, often rusty coloured beneath; petiole very short;
