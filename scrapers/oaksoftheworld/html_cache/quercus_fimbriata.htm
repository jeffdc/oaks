Quercus fimbriata
Author Y.C.Hsu et H.Wei Jen 1976 Acta Phytotax. Sin. 14(2): 86
Synonyms x fimbriata (Hsu & Jen) Chun & Huang 1976
Local names
Range China (Sichuan, Yunnan)
Growth habit
Leaves yellow trichomes on the abaxial face, not covering the secondary veins, of very slightly;
Flowers
Fruits cup with thin, lanceolate to linear scales 4-5 cm long or more;
