  Quercus x almeidae
Author Vila-Viçosa, Capelo, P. Alves & F.M. Vázquez 2022
Synonyms pseudococcifera X rotundifolia
Local names
Range Portugalk; West Andalusia;
Growth habit reaches 6 m tall;
Leaves

3-5(-7) cm x 1-3(-4); concolorous, dark green; obovale to oblong, sometimes lanceolate; margin dentate, spiny; adaxially glabrescent; abaxially pubescent, with short rayed (160 µm) stellate trichomes, as in Q. rotundifolia.

Flowers
Fruits cup with scales intricate and appressed at first, becoming free when mature, mainly the basal ones; maturing same year;

Bark, twigs and
buds

Hardiness zone, habitat among parents; in wet zones;
