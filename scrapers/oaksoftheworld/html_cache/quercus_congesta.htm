Local names
Range Sardinia; Sicily; S Italy; S France;
Growth habit
Leaves 5-8.5 x 3-5 cm; deciduous; elliptical to oblong; base subcordate; 5-8 pairs of regular, rounded lobes, sometimes lobulate and mucronate; dull adaxially; grey green pubescent beneath; tertiary veins evident; petiole 4-14 mm long;
Flowers male flower with 8-9 stamens longer than the perianth; female catkin 1-5 cm long, bearing 3-8 flowers;
Fruits

acorn 2-3 cm long, 1-1.5 cm wide; on a 1-4 cm long stalk, 1 to 5 together; cup cyathiform, 10-15 mm in diameter, with flat scales slightly appressed, covering 1/4 of nut;

Bark, twigs and
buds

bark brown to blackish, shallowly furrowed;
Hardiness zone, habitat not quite hardy (zone 7-8);
Miscellaneous -- Sub-genus Quercus, Section Quercus, Series Roburoid;
-- Closely related to Q.pubescens, bur for Govaerts (and others) it is a true species.
