  Quercus x sterilis
Author Trel. ex E.J.Palmer 1917 Proc. Amer. Philos. Soc. 56: 51
Synonyms marilandica x nigra
Local names
Range Georgia, North Carolina, Texas;
Growth habit
Leaves obovate or oblong-obovate; margin entire or with 3-5 lobes in apical half; glabrous adaxially; slightly pubescent beneath; veins conspicuous;
Flowers
Fruits acorn ovate; cup obconical with loose, puberulent scales;
